# SnapCinema Studio — pre-launch legal review checklist

**Not legal advice.** This document is an **internal checklist** of topics to raise with **qualified, jurisdiction-specific counsel** before any **public** launch involving **stakes**, **yield**, **advertiser metrics**, pooled funds, or **user-generated content** monetization. Do not treat this as a substitute for a lawyer.

---

## When to engage counsel

| Trigger | Action |
|---------|--------|
| **Before mainnet** with real user funds | Product counsel in **each target market** (often US + EU + UK minimum if global). |
| **Yield-for-access**, **staking for streaming**, or **pooled yield** | Securities / consumer financial product review — **not** only “crypto lawyer” generically. |
| **Judge stakes** on creative outcomes | **Prediction market / wagering** and **gambling** regimes vary; framing matters. |
| **Advertiser escrow** tied to **views** | Advertising law, **disclosure**, **dispute** processes, **metrics** representations. |
| **IP lawyer marketplace** on-platform | Professional rules, **platform liability**, **UGC** and **DMCA**-style processes. |
| **Crowdfunding / royalties to backers** | **Securities** and **crowdfunding** rules (e.g. Reg CF / equivalents) if applicable. |

---

## Topics to prepare for counsel (non-exhaustive)

1. **Token and staking framing** — Are “sacrifice yield for access,” **stake-to-curate / stake up-down on content**, or “stake to judge” structures regulated as securities, gambling, wagering, or consumer credit-like products in target jurisdictions?
2. **Custody and DeFi routing** — Who holds keys, who routes to Kamino/Marginfi-like protocols, and what disclosures are required?
3. **UGC and IP** — Terms for derivative works, **DMCA** / notice-and-takedown, repeat infringer policy, **licensing** for “canonical” scenes.
4. **Advertising and endorsements** — Product placement bounties, **FTC**-style disclosure if US-facing.
5. **Data and privacy** — Analytics, **watch patterns**, **clustering**; GDPR/CCPA if applicable.
6. **Dispute resolution** — Escrow release conditions when advertisers dispute **view counts**.

---

## Completion criteria (for this repo)

“Legal review” in project management terms means:

- [ ] **Counsel engaged** with written **engagement letter** and **conflict check**.
- [ ] **Written memo or call notes** on the **above topics** for your **intended v1 feature set** (Phase A vs full SnapCinema Studio).
- [ ] **Terms of Service / Privacy Policy** drafted or reviewed to match **actual** product behavior.
- [ ] **Go/no-go** recorded for **yield-for-access** and **staked judging** before those ship.

Update this checklist with dates and counsel firm **in your private ops docs** (not necessarily in git if you prefer).
